ABOUT CH'EATS

This websites main goal is to empower the user to choose the best prices for the food of their choice from the cheapest . This is obtained by taking the food and delivery prices from the four most popular food delivery services: Deliveroo, Uber Eats, Menulog and Doordash, and displaying them to you in an easy to analyse manner to inform your choices.

How you can use ch'eats to ur advantage.

Ch'eats allows you to do every part of the food delivery application: from choosing the type of food you want, to picking the restaurant and then finally the cheapest price.
